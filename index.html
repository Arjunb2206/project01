About TravelLux

Our Story

Founded in 2020, TravelLux began with a simple mission: to transform ordinary travel into extraordinary experiences. Our founder, Elena Rodriguez, after years of personal travel disappointments, decided to create a service that would focus on personalized, seamless travel experiences.

Over the past decade, we've grown from a small team of three passionate travelers to an international company with offices in London, New York, and Singapore. We've helped thousands of clients create memories that last a lifetime.
